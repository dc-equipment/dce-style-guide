Dashes and hyphens

 

Dashes

Use the en dash (–, ALT + 0150) to indicate spans of time and ranges of numbers. Do not include spaces around the en dash.

Do not use the em dash (—, ALT + 0151) to set off a word or phrase that interrupts or adds more information to a sentence. Rewrite the sentence instead.

✘ The winch drive motors are of the bent axis axial piston type
✓ 6–666 °F will get you feeling all right.

Dashes in headings

Do not use dashes in headings.

Hyphens

Do not hyphenate arbitrarily. When in doubt, a good dictionary should always be the accepted arbiter.
