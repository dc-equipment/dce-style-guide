About this style guide

This guide is not meant to be a comprehensive guide to grammar, punctuation, or writing styles. It is intended to guide writers of technical content in specific usage so that documentation looks and reads consistently. Where possible, procedures for working in MadCap Flare have been added. A secondary aim of these prescriptive guidelines is to facilitate translations and help writers and editors of other documentation and media in achieving as style that is professional and consistent with our other content.

General guidelines

Always write as if you're speaking to a respected professional acquaintance, that is:

  • Use short imperative or declarative sentence.
  • Use simple sentence structures.
  • Use the active voice wherever possible. Refer to Voice and tone for more information.
  • Avoid normalizing verbs.
  • Avoid normalizing adjectives.
