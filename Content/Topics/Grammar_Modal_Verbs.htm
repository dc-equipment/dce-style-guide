Modal or auxiliary verbs

Modal auxiliary are often used to express the writer's attitudes towards the subject matter at hand. However, the ANSI standard prescribes the use of three modal verbs signaling intent. Reserve the use of modal verbs to only safety message and strictly avoid them elsewhere.

Modal verbs and ANSI conventions

The ANSI standard prescribes the following modifiers for imperatives.

  • Shall: A mandatory action or instruction.
    • Thou shall not steal.
  • Should: An advisory action or instruction.
    • Thou should really exercise.
  • May: A permissive action.
    • Thou may downshift a gear or two.
