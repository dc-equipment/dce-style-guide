Capitalization

Use sentence-case capitalization in general, for headings and titles. That is, only capitalize the first word and proper nouns.

Headings and titles

  • Use sentence-style capitalization for all headings and subheadings and no end punctuation.
  • Capitalize the first letter of titles in references, including cross-references, book titles, and external resources.
  • Use gerunds in headings if required, but ensure that you maintain a parallel structure always.
  • Keep headings short and to the point.
  • Place the subject or the most important part of the heading at the beginning (that is, front-load the heading title).
  • Avoid having two headings in a row without text in between them.
