Publication types

end-user and technical documents address the needs of different audiences. See Audience analysis for more information.

Reuse content in multiple documents when required, but avoid duplicating the same content for minor variations.

Operator's manual

Purpose: These manuals contain all the information that an operator may require to use the product, including any setup, operating, and troubleshooting information.

Audience profile: Owners and operators of products. Primarily concerned with understanding the product's functionality for its intended purpose and completing their daily work safely and effectively. Secondary concerns include the ability to fix their machines and maintain them good working condition.

Scope:

  • Product-specific safety information
  • Product description
  • Dimensions and specifications
  • Setup instructions
  • Operating instructions
  • Maintenance and managed maintenance schedules
  • Troubleshooting

Service manual

Purpose: Provides instructions for the service technician to service the product on site.

Audience profile: Authorized service technicians who fulfill maintenance service contracts and fix machine breakage on-site. Primarily concerned with component-level technical detail and the requirements of each maintenance schedule. Secondary concerns include capturing maintenance procedures that can be shared across the service team and advanced troubleshooting.

Scope:

  • Component layout and description
  • Product dimensions and specifications
  • Detailed component specifications
  • Scheduled maintenance details
  • Maintenance procedures
  • Specific maintenance tasks
  • Component technical data

Technical manual

Purpose: Provides the distributors and service technicians with additional technical data and instructions to repair or completely overhaul the product.

Audience profile: Distributors and authorized service technicians. The primary requirement is for a comprehensive, technically detailed specification of the product for warranty and servicing.

Scope:

  • Component layout and description
  • Product dimensions and specifications
  • Detailed component specifications
  • Theory of operation
  • Adjustment and repair
  • Troubleshooting
  • Diagnostics

Workshop manual

Purpose: Provides instructions to dismantle the product and conduct full repairs.

Audience profile: Authorized service technicians who fulfill maintenance service contracts and fix machine breakage on-site. Primarily concerned with fully disassembly and replacement of and third-party product components.

Scope:

  • Workbench instructions
  • Major component repair and replacement
  • Component calibration
  • Jig setup
