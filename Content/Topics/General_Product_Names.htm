Product names

DC Equipment's forestry products are marketed under the Falcon Forestry Equipment brand. Refer to brand guidelines for more information.

Only explicitly state the full name of the product in general sections of the manuals (that is, sections that are reused across different publications) and on the front cover.

There is no need to capitalize the short name of the product.
