Trademarks

A trademark is a word or phrase that is used by the company to identify a product or service. Trademarks are identified by the ™ symbol.

A primary trademark is used for the life of the product.

A secondary trademark is usually a phrase whose life is limited to the life of a product or service.

Primary trademark

DC Equipment and Falcon Forestry Equipment are the primary trademarks.

Secondary trademark

The future in forestry is the secondary trademark.
