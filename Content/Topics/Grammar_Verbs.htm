Verbs

Use action verbs to keep you prose crisp and constructive.

Avoid weak verbs such as is, are, has, have, do, does, provide, support, and so on.

Avoid starting a sentence with "There is ..." or "There are".

Gerunds and participles

Use gerunds and participles, except when clarity is compromised.

Modal or auxiliary verbs

 

Modal verbs and ANSI conventions

The ANSI standard prescribes the following modifiers for imperatives.

  • Shall: A mandatory action or instruction.
    • Thou shall not steal.
  • Should: An advisory action or instruction.
    • Thou should really exercise.
  • May:
    • Thou may downshift a gear or two.
